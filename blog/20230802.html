The web has gotten boring and most sites are over-complicated.

What happened to the old days when people would just upload a couple of simple html files with some interesting CSS styles?

I'm not sure what types of posts I'll be putting on this site, but there's something satisfying about spinning up a simple and efficient site in a handful of minutes where I can say anything I want to anyone in the world.

We shouldn't need react, vue, LLMs, twitter, facebook, and a thousand layers of indirection and abstraction to post on the bulletin board of the internet and connect with other people.
